What is technical explanation?A technical explanation is a way of formally expressing an explanation as a step-by-step set of steps. A technical explanation is one that can be formalized in such a way. It captures what an 'explanation really is.' What good is a formalized explanation?How this can help you explain things to other people. I think that something that is explainable can, from the outside, be understood and built on. I think the more technical your explanation is, the more likely others will be to follow it and build on it.


I think that something something technical explanation or formalization or modeling is probably something a lot better than other kinds of explanations, and can probably be better than explanation in the absence of some formalization. 'We' have done a lot of technical explanations that we still use on a day-to-day level.
