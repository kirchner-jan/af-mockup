This is a transcript of an interview I had with Alexander Kruel for my episode of the __Alignment Newsletter__. In it, we discuss how much complexity in the brain could be explained by "modularity", in particular how much of that comes from the modular structure of connectome-specific harmonic waves (CSHWs), a form of synchrony in the brain's information-processing. Also, we discuss a possible future for our study of the brain, especially in the field of AI safety.
